"Burn-to-Earn"

(1) Intro Description ...

  1. this is a support token for pLUSD that allows you to earn LUSDst while burning pLUSD, etc.

(2) Deposit Instructions ...

  1. Send PLS (not $WPLS) to this contract address and the contract will swap it for USD stable and maintain a balance for you displayed in this dapp. Then you can use that balance in this dapp to easily buy & burn pLUSD, while minting yourself LUSDst

(3) Burn Instructions ...

- connect wallet and view your USD balance
- enter amount of USD you would like to spend to buy & burn $pLUSD
- enter the address of where you would like your reward $LUSDst minted to
- click the 'burn & mint' button
